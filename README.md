# 🌿 AareyKey_NGO

> A collaborative full-stack NGO website for **wildlife rescue, environmental conservation, volunteer coordination, and community awareness in Aarey Forest, Mumbai**.

Aarey Key Foundation is an NGO working in **Aarey Forest, Mumbai**, focused on **wildlife protection and environmental conservation**.  
It conducts **animal rescue operations, handles wildlife emergencies, and collaborates with volunteers and local communities**.

The organization aims to:
- 🐾 Protect biodiversity
- 🌱 Maintain ecological balance
- 🚨 Manage wildlife rescue emergencies
- 🌳 Promote tree plantation drives
- 📢 Spread environmental awareness

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
NGOs working in Aarey Forest face significant challenges in managing **wildlife rescue operations** due to the lack of a **centralized digital system**.

Rescue details, animal records, and rescuer information are mostly handled manually, which often leads to:
- inefficiencies in rescue coordination
- delayed response times
- difficulty tracking rescue history
- poor volunteer communication
- high risk of data loss

This platform solves these issues by digitizing the entire workflow into a **real-time centralized rescue and NGO management system**.

---

## 🚀 Features
- 🔐 User Authentication & Role-Based Access
- 🐾 Wildlife Rescue Case Management
- 🗂️ Centralized Database Management
- 🚨 Emergency Alert & Notification System
- 🤝 Volunteer Management System
- 🌳 Plantation & Environmental Activity Records
